Attractions

Kangar, the capital of Perlis in Malaysia, is a charming town known for its serene landscapes and cultural attractions. Nestled in the northernmost state of Malaysia, Kangar offers a variety of attractions that appeal to nature lovers, history enthusiasts, and those seeking a peaceful retreat.

Kota Kayang Museum.

Location: Jalan Marhum Kayang, Kuala Perlis 02000, Malaysia Open: Tuesday - Sunday Time: 9:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m. Close: Monday Fee: Free

Kelam Cave

Location: Kaki Bukit, Perlis Malaysia Open: Everyday (it may change on public holiday or special occasions) Time: Monday - Friday (9:00 a.m. - 5:30 p.m.) Saturday - Sunday (9:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m.) Fee: Adult - RM 2.00 Children (4 years - 12 years old) - 0.50 cent
