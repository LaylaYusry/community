KANGAR, PERLIS

Kangar is located in Perlis, Malaysia. It is situated between five territories: Sena, Kechor, Wang Bintong, Seriab, and Utan Aji.
Kangar serves as the administrative capital of the state of Perlis Indera Kayangan and is also known as the principal commercial capital. Kangar is the most densely developed area in this region. Its history dates back 350 years to the construction of Sena, which was the administrative center for the 14th Sultan of Kedah, Sultan Muhyiddin Mansor Shah. Over the years, Kangar has undergone rapid development, with new buildings replacing old ones. However, some old and historic buildings have been preserved and serve as landmarks of Kangar, including the Bangunan Jam Besar, now the Menteri Besar Office Building, and the Alwi Mosque.
