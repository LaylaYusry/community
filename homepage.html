KANGAR, PERLIS

Kangar is located in Perlis, Malaysia.
It is situated between five territories: Sena, Kechor, Wang Bintong, Seriab, and Utan Aji.
Kangar serves as the administrative capital of the state of Perlis Indera Kayangan and is also known as the principal commercial capital.
