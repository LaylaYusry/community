CULTURE

The influence of Thai and Malay dominates the state of Perlis. The most common traditional practices that people indulge in include traditional dances, games, and many other interesting entertainments. If you are visiting Perlis just after the harvest period, you will see the traditional colourful music and dance performances known as the Tarian Canggung. Another performance that most visitors and the locals love is the Tarian Terinai, a traditional dance performed during royal ceremonies.

Tarian Canggung is one of the most vibrant traditional dances in Perlis. The dance is characterized by its lively music and colorful attire, making it a visual and auditory delight for spectators.

Tarian Terinai is another significant traditional dance in Perlis, often performed during royal ceremonies. It showcases the rich cultural heritage and graceful movements of the local people.

Traditional games are an integral part of Perlis culture, offering fun and entertainment rooted in local traditions. These games often bring communities together in joyous celebrations.

The traditional costumes of Perlis reflect a blend of Thai and Malay influences, showcasing vibrant colors and intricate designs that are symbolic of cultural pride and identity.

The local cuisine of Perlis is a delightful fusion of Thai and Malay flavors, offering a unique gastronomic experience that reflects the region's cultural diversity and heritage.

Traditional music plays a vital role in Perlis culture, enriching ceremonies and celebrations with melodious tunes that resonate with the region's rich cultural heritage and traditions.
